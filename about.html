About Us

Profile of our founding partner


Anup Luharuka

Anup is a commerce graduate from Calcutta University (CU).  He is a Fellow Chartered Accountant (FCA) as well as Fellow Company Secretary (FCS) from India.

His experience spans over 16 years across multiple functions spreading over accounting, auditing, taxation and company law. He is helping clients across industries.

In addition to audit and accounting, he is an expert in income tax and related matters, dealing with the tax department across levels including tax scrutiny cases, internal audits, company law compliance and secretarial matters. He has successfully provided CFO support to various companies in the past. He is an expert in transitioning client in-house accounting services to outsourcing agency and provide huge cost efficiency to the clients. 

Anup is actively involved preparing article on significant/upcoming matters in various accounting journals and has also been  extensively involved in various training programs on various accounting matters. He is also part of our CFO Desk providing CFO services to our clients.
