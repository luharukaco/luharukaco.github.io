About Luharuka & Co

Luharuka & Co., a partnership firm established under regulation of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), was established in India by a group of bright professionals under the leadership  of Mr. Anup Luharuka. Our founding partner has more than 16 years of experience in the area of accounting, advisory, auditing and taxation.


At Luharuka & Co, we provide one stop solution to our clients, with a range of accounting, auditing and consulting services including financial and business advisory. To bring services to our client’s doorsteps, we have established our associate office across India. We also have our associate offices across India and USA.

Currently we have a team of experienced professional leaders with extensive experience in the field of accounting, auditing and consulting services including financial and business advisory as well as industry expertize. They are supported by a group of bright professionals to help the clients in their business needs.

We always look forward to innovative methods to solve the complex problems of our valued clients. We are a professionally managed, service oriented and knowledge based company. We firmly believe in absolute transparency in all our dealings with our clients. The professional approach of our company in rendering specialized services is well recognized.
